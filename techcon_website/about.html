History

TechCon was established in 2010 as a small local meetup for software developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into an internationally recognized event, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Mission

Our mission is to connect innovators, developers, and leaders in the tech industry to inspire, learn, and shape the future together. TechCon promotes open knowledge sharing, diversity, and forward-thinking technology solutions.
